## Recurring Transactions

Automate your regular income and expenses to save time and never miss a transaction.

### Setting Up a Recurring Transaction

1. Navigate to **Recurring** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Recurring Transaction**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Amount**: Transaction amount
   - **Category**: Select appropriate category
   - **Description**: Brief description
   - **Type**: Income or Expense
   - **Frequency**: How often it repeats (daily, weekly, monthly, etc.)
   - **Start Date**: When it begins
   - **End Date** (optional): When it stops
4. Enable **Reminders** if you want notifications before it occurs
5. Click **Save**

### Managing Recurring Transactions

- **View All**: See all your recurring transactions with next occurrence dates
- **Edit**: Click the edit icon to modify details
- **Pause**: Toggle the switch to temporarily pause without deleting
- **Delete**: Remove the recurring transaction permanently

**üí° Tip**: Use recurring transactions for rent, salary, subscriptions, and regular bills to automate your financial tracking.

## Smart Budget Recommendations

Let AI help you optimize your spending with personalized budget suggestions.

### Generating Recommendations

1. Navigate to **Budgets**
2. Click **Get AI Recommendations**
3. SmartWallet analyzes your spending history (requires 30+ days of data)
4. Review suggested budget limits for each category

### Understanding Recommendations

Each recommendation shows:
- **Suggested Limit**: AI-recommended budget amount
- **Current Spending**: Your recent average
- **Potential Savings**: How much you could save
- **Confidence Level**: How confident the AI is (High/Medium/Low)
- **Reasoning**: Why this recommendation was made

### Accepting or Dismissing

- **Accept**: Click "Accept" to create/update the budget with suggested amount
- **Customize**: Edit the amount before accepting
- **Dismiss**: Click "Dismiss" if you don't want this recommendation
- **Accept All**: Apply all recommendations at once

**üí° Tip**: Start with high-confidence recommendations and adjust based on your lifestyle needs.

## Receipt Scanning

Reduce manual data entry by scanning receipts with OCR technology.

### Scanning a Receipt

1. Navigate to **Transactions**
2. Click **Scan Receipt**
3. Choose upload method:
   - **Upload File**: Select image from your device
   - **Take Photo**: Use your camera (mobile)
4. Adjust the image if needed (crop, rotate)
5. Click **Process Receipt**

### Reviewing Extracted Data

After processing, SmartWallet extracts:
- **Merchant Name**: Store or vendor
- **Date**: Transaction date
- **Total Amount**: Receipt total
- **Line Items**: Individual purchases (if available)

**Confidence Indicators**:
- ‚úÖ **High Confidence** (>80%): Likely accurate
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Low Confidence** (<80%): Review and edit

### Saving the Transaction

1. Review extracted data
2. Edit any incorrect fields
3. Select category
4. Add notes if needed
5. Click **Save Transaction**

The original receipt image is attached to the transaction for future reference.

**üí° Tip**: Take clear, well-lit photos for best OCR results. Flatten receipts to avoid shadows.

## Investment Tracking

Monitor your investment portfolio with growth projections and performance analytics.

### Adding an Investment

1. Navigate to **Investments**
2. Click **Add Investment**
3. Enter details:
   - **Name**: Investment name (e.g., "Apple Stock")
   - **Type**: Stock, Bond, ETF, Crypto, etc.
   - **Symbol** (optional): Ticker symbol
   - **Initial Amount**: Original investment
   - **Current Value**: Present value
   - **Rate Per Annum**: Expected annual return rate
   - **Purchase Date**: When you bought it
   - **Notes** (optional): Additional information
4. Click **Save**

### Understanding Performance Metrics

For each investment, SmartWallet calculates:
- **Total Return**: Gain/loss amount
- **Total Return %**: Percentage gain/loss
- **Annualized Return**: Average yearly return
- **Projected Values**: Future value at 1, 3, and 5 years

### Portfolio Dashboard

View your complete portfolio:
- **Total Portfolio Value**: Sum of all investments
- **Total Return**: Overall gain/loss
- **Asset Allocation**: Pie chart showing distribution
- **Growth Chart**: Historical performance over time

### Updating Investment Values

1. Find the investment in your list
2. Click **Update Value**
3. Enter new current value
4. Click **Save**

All metrics recalculate automatically.

**üí° Tip**: Update investment values monthly to track accurate performance. Use the rate per annum to project realistic growth.

## Debt Management

Track your debts and visualize your path to becoming debt-free.

### Adding a Debt

1. Navigate to **Debt Tracker**
2. Click **Add Debt**
3. Enter details:
   - **Name**: Debt name (e.g., "Student Loan")
   - **Type**: Credit Card, Mortgage, Car Loan, etc.
   - **Original Amount**: Initial debt amount
   - **Current Balance**: Amount still owed
   - **Interest Rate**: Annual percentage rate
   - **Minimum Payment**: Required monthly payment
   - **Due Date**: Payment due date
4. Click **Save**

### Understanding Debt Metrics

SmartWallet calculates:
- **Payoff Date**: When debt will be paid off (at minimum payments)
- **Total Interest**: Interest you'll pay over the life of the debt
- **Months Remaining**: Time until debt-free

### Recording Payments

1. Find the debt in your list
2. Click **Record Payment**
3. Enter payment amount and date
4. SmartWallet automatically:
   - Calculates interest accrued
   - Splits payment between principal and interest
   - Updates remaining balance
   - Recalculates payoff date

### Debt Reduction Analytics

View your progress:
- **Total Debt**: Sum of all debts
- **Monthly Obligations**: Total minimum payments
- **Reduction Timeline**: Chart showing balance over time
- **Accelerated Payoff Calculator**: See impact of extra payments

**üí° Tip**: Use the accelerated payoff calculator to see how extra payments can save you thousands in interest.

## Security & Privacy

Protect your financial data with robust security features.

### Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Add an extra layer of security:

1. Navigate to **Settings** ‚Üí **Security**
2. Click **Enable MFA**
3. Choose method:
   - **Authenticator App**: Use Google Authenticator or similar
   - **Email Code**: Receive codes via email
   - **SMS Code**: Receive codes via text
4. Follow setup instructions
5. Save backup codes in a secure location

### Password Management

**Changing Your Password**:
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password (must meet requirements)
5. Confirm new password
6. Click **Update**

**Password Requirements**:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

### Session Management

View and manage active sessions:
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Security** ‚Üí **Active Sessions**
2. See all devices where you're logged in
3. Click **Logout** on any session to end it
4. Use **Logout All Sessions** to sign out everywhere

### Auto-Logout

SmartWallet automatically logs you out after 30 minutes of inactivity for security.

### Data Encryption

Your sensitive financial data is encrypted:
- **At Rest**: AES-256 encryption in database
- **In Transit**: TLS 1.3 encryption for all communications

### Privacy Controls

Manage your data:
- **Export Data**: Download all your financial data
- **Delete Account**: Permanently remove your account and data
- **Data Sharing**: Control what data is used for analytics

**üí° Tip**: Enable MFA for maximum account security. Use a password manager to create strong, unique passwords.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Problem**: OCR not extracting receipt data accurately
- **Solution**: Ensure good lighting, flatten receipt, try re-uploading

**Problem**: Recurring transaction not creating automatically
- **Solution**: Check that it's marked as "Active" and the date has passed

**Problem**: Budget recommendations not appearing
- **Solution**: Ensure you have at least 30 days of transaction history

**Problem**: Financial health score seems incorrect
- **Solution**: Verify all transactions, budgets, and debts are entered correctly

**Problem**: Not receiving notifications
- **Solution**: Check notification preferences and browser/device settings
