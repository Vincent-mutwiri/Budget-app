# AI Assistant Context Setup Guide

This guide ensures your AI Assistant has complete financial data to provide personalized, contextualized advice.

## Overview

The AI Assistant uses **RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)** to analyze your financial data and provide tailored recommendations. For best results, complete all sections below.

---

## ‚úÖ Setup Checklist

### 1. **Main Account Balance** (Required)
Your primary savings/checking account that funds everything.

**How to set up:**
1. Go to **Accounts** page
2. Your Main Account is automatically created
3. Add your current balance by creating an income transaction or editing the account

**Why it matters:** AI needs to know your available funds for investment, debt payoff, and goal recommendations.

---

### 2. **Transactions** (Highly Recommended)
Your income and expenses provide spending patterns.

**Minimum recommended:** 30 days of transactions

**How to add:**
1. Go to **Transactions** page
2. Add transactions manually or use **Scan Receipt** feature
3. Include:
   - ‚úÖ All income sources (salary, freelance, etc.)
   - ‚úÖ Regular expenses (rent, utilities, groceries)
   - ‚úÖ Discretionary spending (entertainment, dining)

**Why it matters:** AI analyzes spending patterns to suggest budget optimizations and identify savings opportunities.

---

### 3. **Budgets** (Highly Recommended)
Monthly spending limits by category.

**Minimum recommended:** 3-5 budget categories

**How to set up:**
1. Go to **Budgets** page
2. Click **Add New Budget**
3. Set limits for key categories:
   - Groceries
   - Transportation
   - Entertainment
   - Utilities
   - Dining Out

**Why it matters:** AI compares actual spending vs. budgets to provide actionable advice on overspending and savings.

---

### 4. **Savings Goals** (Recommended)
Financial targets you're working towards.

**Examples:**
- Emergency Fund: KSh 100,000
- Vacation: KSh 50,000
- New Laptop: KSh 80,000
- House Deposit: KSh 500,000

**How to set up:**
1. Go to **Goals** page
2. Click **Add New Goal**
3. Set target amount and deadline
4. Contribute from Main Account

**Why it matters:** AI prioritizes recommendations based on your goals and suggests optimal contribution strategies.

---

### 5. **Debts** (If Applicable)
Outstanding loans and credit obligations.

**Include:**
- Credit cards
- Personal loans
- Student loans
- Mortgages
- Car loans

**How to set up:**
1. Go to **Debt Tracker** page
2. Click **Add New Debt**
3. Enter:
   - Original amount
   - Current balance
   - Interest rate
   - Minimum payment
   - Due date

**Why it matters:** AI calculates payoff strategies, suggests debt consolidation, and prioritizes high-interest debt.

---

### 6. **Investments** (If Applicable)
Your investment portfolio and assets.

**Include:**
- Stocks
- Bonds
- Mutual Funds
- Real Estate
- Retirement Accounts (401k, IRA)
- Crypto

**How to set up:**
1. Go to **Investments** page
2. Click **Add New Investment**
3. Enter:
   - Investment name
   - Type
   - Initial amount
   - Current value
   - Rate of return

**Why it matters:** AI provides portfolio diversification advice and suggests rebalancing strategies.

## üÜò Troubleshooting

**AI gives generic advice?**

- Set up budgets
- Add at least one goal or debt

**AI doesn't mention my investments?**
- Ensure investments are added in Investments page
- Update current values

**AI doesn't consider my goals?**
- Create goals in Goals page
- Add target amounts and deadlines
